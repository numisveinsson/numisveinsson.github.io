On October 25th 2025, I joined the Predictive Modeling in Medicine Lab (PMML), led by Prof. Charley Taylor, as a [postdoctoral researcher](https://oden.utexas.edu/people/directory/numi-cepero-2465/). The lab is part of the [Center for Computational Medicine](https://oden.utexas.edu/research/centers-and-groups/center-for-computational-medicine/) at the Oden Institute and Dell Medical School, The University of Texas at Austin.
