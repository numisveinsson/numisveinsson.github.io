Gave an oral presentation on **“Sub-Voxel Vascular Segmentation from Coarse Resolution Medical Imaging”** at the [10th World Congress of Biomechanics (WCB 2026)](https://wcb2026.com/) in Vancouver, Canada. Presented in session **OA144 — Image Based Cardiovascular Biomechanics**.

Work with Dr. Julie DiCarlo, Prof. Shawn Shadden, and Prof. Charles Taylor (UT Austin / Oden Institute and UC Berkeley).
